Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to your most common questions about Oldwyn products, orders, shipping, and more.

Orders & Payments

You can place an order directly on our website by Browse our collection, selecting your desired Senator attire, choosing the size and color, and adding it to your cart. Follow the steps through our secure checkout process to complete your purchase.

We accept major credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and other secure online payment gateways. All transactions are processed securely.

We process orders quickly to ensure prompt delivery. If you need to modify or cancel your order, please contact our customer service team immediately at support@oldwyn.com. We will do our best to accommodate your request, but cannot guarantee changes once processing has begun.

Shipping & Delivery

We offer complimentary global shipping on all orders. Expedited shipping options may be available at an additional cost, which will be calculated at checkout based on your location.

Standard delivery typically takes 7-14 business days, depending on your location. You will receive a tracking number once your order has shipped, allowing you to monitor its progress.

Yes, Oldwyn proudly offers complimentary global shipping to most countries worldwide. Please note that customs duties and taxes may apply upon arrival in certain regions, which are the responsibility of the customer.

Products & Sizing

We provide a detailed Size Guide on each product page. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing it to our chart for the best fit. If you're still unsure, feel free to contact our customer service for personalized advice.

Oldwyn Senator attires are crafted from high-quality fabrics, including premium cotton, linen blends, and other luxurious materials, chosen for their comfort, durability, and elegant drape. Specific material details can be found on each product's page.

Returns & Exchanges

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn, unwashed, and undamaged items with original tags still attached. For full details and instructions, please visit our Shipping & Returns page.

To initiate a return or exchange, please contact our customer service team at support@oldwyn.com with your order number and the reason for your return. They will provide you with detailed instructions and a return authorization.

Still Have Questions?

If you couldn't find the answer you were looking for, please don't hesitate to reach out to our dedicated customer support team.
