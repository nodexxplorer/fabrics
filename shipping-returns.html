Shipping & Returns Policy

At Oldwyn, we are committed to delivering your distinguished Senator attire efficiently and ensuring your satisfaction with every purchase. Please review our policies below.

Shipping Information

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-3 business days after placement. During peak seasons or sales, processing times may be slightly extended.

Shipping Options & Delivery Times

  • Complimentary Global Shipping: We are pleased to offer free standard shipping on all orders worldwide. Delivery usually takes 7-14 business days, depending on your location.
  • Expedited Shipping: For faster delivery, expedited options are available at an additional cost, calculated at checkout. Delivery times for expedited shipping range from 3-7 business days.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email confirmation with a tracking number. You can use this number to monitor the status and estimated delivery date of your package.

International Shipping & Customs

Oldwyn ships globally. Please be aware that for international orders, customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or import fees that may be levied by the destination country's customs authorities. These charges are not included in your order total and are beyond our control.

Returns & Exchanges

Our 7-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

We want you to be completely satisfied with your Oldwyn attire. If you are not entirely happy with your purchase, you may return or exchange eligible items within 7 days of delivery.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, undamaged, and in their original condition.
  • All original tags, packaging, and accessories must be intact.
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable" are not eligible for returns or exchanges.

How to Initiate a Return or Exchange

  1. Contact our Customer Support Team at support@oldwyn.com within 7 days of receiving your order.
  2. Provide your order number, the item(s) you wish to return or exchange, and the reason for your request.
  3. Our team will provide you with a Return Authorization (RA) number and detailed instructions on how to send your item back.
  4. Package your item securely, including the RA number inside the package.
  5. Ship the item back to us using a trackable shipping method.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 7-10 business days.

Exchanges

If you wish to exchange an item for a different size or color, please follow the return initiation process and specify your desired exchange. We recommend placing a new order for the desired item to ensure availability, and your original order will be refunded upon return.

Return Shipping Costs

Customers are responsible for the cost of return shipping, unless the return is due to a defective or incorrect item received. In such cases, Oldwyn will cover the return shipping costs.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately (within 48 hours of delivery) with photos of the damage. We will work swiftly to resolve the issue by offering a replacement or full refund, including return shipping costs.

Questions About Your Order?

Our dedicated customer support team is ready to assist you with any questions regarding shipping, returns, or your Oldwyn purchase.
