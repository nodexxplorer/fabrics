Find Your Perfect Fit

At Oldwyn, we want your Senator attire to fit flawlessly. Use our comprehensive size guide to ensure you select the perfect size every time.

How to Measure

1. Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the measuring tape horizontal.

2. Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, typically the narrowest part, above your navel.

3. Sleeve Length

Measure from the center back of your neck, across your shoulder, down to your wrist.

4. Garment Length

Measure from the highest point of the shoulder down to where you want the garment to end.

Fit Tips & Recommendations

  • Classic Fit: Our Senator attires are designed for a comfortable, classic fit that drapes elegantly on the body.
  • Between Sizes: If your measurements fall between two sizes, we recommend choosing the larger size for a more relaxed fit, or the smaller size for a more tailored, snug look.
  • Fabric Stretch: While our fabrics offer comfort, they generally have minimal stretch. Consider this when choosing between sizes.
  • Model Reference: Please refer to the product descriptions for details on the size worn by our models, which can help you gauge the fit.
